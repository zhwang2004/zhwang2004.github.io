This paper introduces **APPBoost**, an adaptive parameter-pair boosting algorithm designed to enhance the robustness of machine learning models against noise and class imbalance. The method innovatively introduces an adaptive parameter adjustment mechanism, significantly improving classification accuracy and model performance in noisy and imbalanced environments. The results show that APPBoost outperforms existing algorithms, including traditional AdaBoost, in terms of robustness and prediction accuracy.
